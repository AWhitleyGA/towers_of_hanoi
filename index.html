Towers of Hanoi

Instructions:

Towers of Hanoi is a mathematical puzzle game designed by Edouard Lucas in 1883.

The objective of the game is to move all of the disks from one pole to another.

Rules:

  • You can only move one disk at a time.
  • You may not place a disk on top of a disk of a smaller size
